The Challenges of Working Out

While working out is rewarding, it comes with its own set of challenges:

  • Risk of overtraining and injury
  • Struggling with consistency and motivation
  • Body dysmorphia and comparison with others
  • Balancing workout routines with daily responsibilities

It's important to acknowledge these challenges and find effective ways to overcome them. Rest, self-awareness, and a balanced approach can help you stay on track.
